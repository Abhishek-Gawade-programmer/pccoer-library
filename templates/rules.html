Rules & Regulation

General Rules


  • Every student must possess his/ her Library Card while making use of the Library facility .
  • Two Books will be issued against one Library Card.
  • Books will be issued to the students for 15 days only. In the last page slip is pasted and stamped mentioning the due date and the fine will be charged @ Re.1 per day per book from the due date till the book is returned to library.
  • Books from the special collection ,dissertation , non-book materials etc are not to be issued outside without the permission of the library at all.
  • Enter your name and Sign in the register kept at the entrance counter before entering library.
  • Library borrower cards are not transferable. The borrower is responsible for the books borrowed on his/her card.
  • Periodicals & Journals are issued against I cards for Reading in the library only.
  • Loud talking , conversations etc . are strictly prohibited in the library.

Loss and Damages


  • Lost book should be replaced by Good/new copy of the latest edition immediately
  • In case the borrower is unable to replace the lost book ,twice the cost of books plus overdue charges will be recovered.
  • Loss of Library card should be reported to the library immediately
  • Duplicate card will be issued on payment of Rs.50/-.
  • Use the books carefully and do not mark them or tear down the pages.
  • Protect the books from rain, insects and dust.
  • Book in the damaged condition will not be accepted that needs to be replaced
